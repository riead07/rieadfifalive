🛠️ Secure Live Setup Instructions

Follow these steps to cast your OBS stream securely from your PC directly to your RieadLiveFIFA website on Render.

Step 01

OBS Output Config

Open OBS. Go to Settings > Stream. Select Twitch as the service. Connect your Twitch account or paste your Stream Key, and click Start Streaming.

Step 02

Deploying to Render

Go to Render, link this GitHub repository, and click Deploy. Your website will be live in 2 minutes.

Step 03

View Live on Web

Open your website, type your Twitch username in the control panel, click Connect, and watch your OBS cast in premium 4K!
